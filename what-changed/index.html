“What changed?”

July 24, 2025

Some days ago, you asked me, “What changed?” After you pointed out that I’ve been saying I’m tired lately — and that I didn’t used to say that. It got me thinking. And the short answer is: I don’t know. I’m not exactly sure what changed.

The long answer is, I don’t know — but… I have some suspicions. Something to do with long-lost memories returning to me. When I wake up in the morning, my thoughts are racing. So probably at night, my thoughts are racing too. Probably, this is not conducive to a good night’s rest. I’m not sure why my thoughts are racing. But I do know that memories have been surfacing. Memories that were long long forgotten. Things I hadn’t thought of in years. Forgotten fragments from far away.

Or maybe it’s just sleep debt quietly piling up. Or the fact that I don’t really take time to relax during the day. Or that I don’t wind down before bed. Or that I’ve gone from 3–5 cups of coffee a day to 6, every single day.

So, short answer: I don’t know. Long answer is the same, but with some suspicions.

Thanks for the insight, Paula.
